Why Choose LPG One?

LPG Gas safety certificate and regulation in London and in the UK

As a business or a landlord with LPG installation, you have responsibilities under the Health & Safety at Work Etc Act (HSWA) 1974 & the subsidiary legislation to make sure that your installation is safe to operate & does not present a risk to your employees or members of the public. When designing you instalation, get the help of an LPG certified engineer to define where and how to set in place your storage tank - think ventilation and accessibility, and your pipework - here think of pressure & distance. You would need to make a suitable and sufficient assessment of the risk that the installation could present.

A LPG Certified person like LPG ONE is required to certify on a regular basis that your instalation is correctly done and do not require upgrade.
